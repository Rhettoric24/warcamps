1. RESOURCE SYSTEM
================================================================================

SPHERES (Currency)
- Primary game currency earned through missions and markets
- Starting amount (Dev): 25,000 S | (Production): 15,000 S
- Income sources:
  * Markets: 400 S per day per market (stackable with fabrials)
  * Scout missions: 500-1,500 S (based on unit carry capacity)
  * Attack missions: 2,000-5,000 S (with gemheart capture chance at 10%)
- Spending: Unit recruitment, building construction, gemheart exchange (10,000 S)

GEMHEARTS (Premium Currency)
- Limited resource obtained through:
  * Attack raids (10% capture chance)
  * Plateau runs (1-2 randomly won)
  * Black market exchange (10,000 S → 1 Gemheart)
- Uses:
  * Shardbearer recruitment (1 per unit)
  * Fabrial construction (1-5 per fabrial type)
- Value: Very high - limits progression without time or real spending

PROVISIONS (Food/Supplies)
- Population cap for military units
- Starting capacity: 150 units
- Increases by:
  * +50 per Soulcast Bunker (stackable)
  * Heatrial fabrials (1.5x multiplier on total cap when owned)
- Minimal spending: 1 provision per military unit per day (soft cap at upkeep penalties)
- Critical constraint: Early game bottleneck for army growth

TERRITORY (Land/Control)
- Starting: 0/50 maximum
- Increases through conquest missions: 10-25 land per victory
- Soft cap at 50 - can exceed but building costs increase
- Strategic gating: More buildings require more territory
- No direct income source - purely strategic resource

2. UNIT SYSTEM
================================================================================

UNIT STATS STRUCTURE:
Each unit has: Power (combat strength) | Survival (casualty resistance) | Carry 
(loot capacity) | Speed (mission duration modifier) | Cost | Provision consumption

MILITARY UNITS:

Bridgecrews (Cost: 5 S)
- Power: 0.1 | Survival: 25% | Carry: 0 | Speed: +0.01 | Provision: 1
- Role: Trash unit, low value, mainly for padding unit count
- Use case: Early game fill, speedrun missions

Spearmen (Cost: 10 S)
- Power: 1.0 | Survival: 75% | Carry: 0 | Speed: 0 | Provision: 1
- Role: Standard infantry, balanced stats
- Use case: Core military backbone

Archers (Cost: 15 S)
- Power: 1.0 | Survival: 90% | Carry: 0 | Speed: 0 | Provision: 1
- Role: Ranged units, best survival rate
- Use case: Defensive/casualty-efficient armies

Chulls (Cost: 50 S)
- Power: 0.5 | Survival: 80% | Carry: 10 | Speed: -0.025 (slows missions) | Provision: 3
- Role: Transport/loot enabler, requires more provisions
- Use case: Scout and attack missions where carry capacity matters

Shardbearers (Cost: 1 Gemheart)
- Power: 2.0x multiplier | Survival: 99% | Carry: 0 | Speed: 0 | Provision: 3
- Role: Elite unit, massive power boost, extremely rare
- Use case: Late-game power scaling, high-stakes missions
- Limitation: Requires gemhearts, gatekeeping resource

SPY UNITS (Separate from military):

Lighteyes Noble (Cost: 50 S)
- Spy Power: 1 | Survival: 50%
- Base for espionage operations

Undercity Spy (Cost: 150 S)
- Spy Power: 2 | Survival: 70%
- Mid-tier espionage agent

Ghostblood Agent (Cost: 600 S)
- Spy Power: 5 | Survival: 90%
- Elite agent, highest impact


UNIT UPKEEP TIERS (Soft penalties):
- 0-200 units: No penalty
- 201-400 units: Sphere costs increase (tier 2)
- 401-600 units: Sphere costs increase further (tier 3)
- 600+ units: Maximum penalty, diminishing returns
Logic: Encourages diverse unit strategies over giant armies

3. BUILDING SYSTEM
================================================================================

BUILDING MECHANICS:
- Costs scale based on quantity owned (exponential/linear)
- Each provides specific bonuses or unlocks
- Land costs: Different buildings consume different territory amounts
- Upkeep: Some buildings cost spheres daily to maintain

INFRASTRUCTURE BUILDINGS:

Spy Network (Cost: 5,000 S | Land: 5 | Max: 1 | Upkeep: 75 S/day)
- Unlocks: Espionage system and agent recruitment
- Gating: Must own 1 to access spy missions
- Strategic: Early investment into espionage path

Research Library (Cost: 10,000 S | Land: 5 | Max: 1)
- Unlocks: Fabrial system and technology
- Gating: Must own 1 to construct fabrials
- Strategic: Required for late-game power scaling

Grand Market (Cost: 250 S | Land: 1 | Scales: Exponential)
- Income: +400 S/day per market
- Multiplier: Synchronized Ledger fabrial (1.5x per owned)
- Economic engine: Primary passive income source

Soulcast Bunkers (Cost: 150 S | Land: 1 | Scales: Exponential)
- Bonus: +50 Provision capacity per bunker
- Multiplier: Heatrial fabrial (1.5x total provision cap per owned)
- Production constraint: Enables larger armies

Military Camp (Cost: 7,000 S | Land: 5 | Scales: Linear)
- Bonus: +10% army power (per camp)
- Stackable: Multiple camps stack linearly (+20%, +30%, etc.)
- Power scaling: Key to late-game military strength

Ardent Monastery (Cost: 7,000 S | Land: 5 | Scales: Linear | Upkeep: 50 S/day)
- Bonus: +5% survival rate (per monastery)
- Stackable: Multiple monasteries stack
- Casualty reduction: Enables missions with lower losses

Stormshelter (Cost: 500 S | Land: 1 | Upkeep: 25 S/day)
- Defense: Protects 1 building from highstorm destruction (5% per building)
- Event response: Required for endgame highstorm protection
- Insurance: Prevents setbacks during storms

The Whisper Tower (Cost: 10 Ghostbloods | Land: 5 | Max: 1)
- Special: Ultimate spy defense and offense building
- Requires: Ghostblood agents to build
- Effect: Provides detection and amplification for spy operations
- Rarity: Only buildable with premium agents

4. MISSION SYSTEM
================================================================================

MISSION ARCHITECTURE:
All missions follow unified deployment pattern:
1. Open deployment modal → 2. Select units → 3. Confirm deploy → 
4. Mission appears in active list → 5. View mission details → 
6. Mission completes → 7. Result modal shows outcome

MISSION TYPES:

SCOUT MISSION (Cyan | ~1 Day)
- Cost: None (free to deploy)
- Mechanics: 
  * Carry score = sum(unit carry values) × Loot Bonus % 
  * Reward: 500-1,500 S based on carry
  * Casualties: 5-10% on success, 20% on failure
- Strategy: Maximize carry capacity (chulls) for better rewards

ATTACK MISSION (Red | ~1.5 Days)
- Cost: None
- Mechanics:
  * Power calculation: sum(unit power) + Training Camp bonus + Shardbearer multiplier
  * Reward: 2,000-5,000 S
  * Gemheart: 10% random capture chance on victory
  * Casualties: 10-15% on success, 30% on failure
- Strategy: Power race, maximize shardbearers for consistency

PLATEAU RUN (Orange | ~2 Days | Coalition Event)
- Cost: None
- Mechanics:
  * Coalition: Multiple players compete simultaneously
  * Winner determination: Speed (duration) and power
  * Reward: Shared loot + potential gemheart to winner
  * Casualties: 40% (highstorm causes massive losses)
- Strategy: Speed multiplication from fabrials, power for competition
- Event driven: Triggers randomly every 3-5 in-game days

CONQUEST MISSION (Cyan | 1 Day)
- Cost: 200 S + 100 Provisions
- Mechanics:
  * Enemy power: 80 + (maxLand - 50) × 2.5
  * Victory determination: playerPower / (playerPower + enemyPower) = win chance
  * Land reward: 10-25 (based on victory margin)
  * Casualties: 8-15% victory, 25-35% defeat
- Strategy: Territory exponential growth, expanding cap-gating

ESPIONAGE MISSION (Purple | Variable)
- Cost: None (agent cost already paid)
- Mechanics:
  * Agent power: sum(agent spy values)
  * Success chance: Agent power vs target defense
  * Target: Choose from 3 NPC princes (each has different stats)
  * Operations: Scan (info), Steal (resources), Sabotage (damage)
  * Risk: Agent capture on failure, growing suspicion
- Strategy: Counter-intel system, suspicion management


MISSION COMPLETION LOGIC:

1. Time-based: Each mission has returnTime = duration × speed modifiers
2. Casualty calculation: 
   - Base casualty % from mission type
   - Modified by Monastery bonus + Survival rates
   - Highstorm doubles casualty rates
3. Reward calculation: Mission-type specific math
4. Return: Forces available again when mission complete

5. UPKEEP & PENALTY SYSTEM
================================================================================

DAILY UPKEEP COSTS:
- Stormshelter: 25 S each per day
- Monastery: 50 S each per day (if built)
- Spy Network: 75 S per day (if built)
- Unit provisions: Minimal (included in provision tracking)

UNIT UPKEEP PENALTIES (Sphere cost multipliers):
Tier 1 (0-200 units): No penalty (100% costs)
Tier 2 (201-400 units): 120% costs
Tier 3 (401-600 units): 150% costs
Tier 4 (600+ units): 200% costs

Logic: Forces strategic balance between unit quantity and economic sustainability

6. HIGHSTORM SYSTEM
================================================================================

RANDOM EVENT with cascading effects:

PHASES:
1. Announcement: 10 second full-screen overlay
2. Warning: 10 seconds to 10 minutes (top banner, clickable)
3. Persistent: 10 minutes to end (small banner)

EFFECTS:

Army Casualties:
- 20-25% to deployed forces
- 40% to plateau run participants
- Chull protection: -5% per chull (max 50% reduction)

Building Destruction (5% chance each):
- Markets and Soulcast Bunkers at risk
- Stormshelters provide 1 protection each
- Requires rebuild via Logistics

Fabrial Overcharge:
- 60% chance: 3x speed boost (Gravity Rigs), 2x use (Arena fabrials)
- 40% chance: Burnout (Gravity Rigs disabled, random arena fabrial destroyed)
- Duration: 3 days

Spy Chaos: +2x spy power for 24 hours

Black Market Discount: 10,000 S → 7,500 S for 48 hours

Mitigation: Mission recall (50% cost of remaining time)

7. FABRIALS (MAGITECH SYSTEM)
================================================================================

FABRIALS are technology multipliers built after Research Library unlocked.
Require Gemhearts (premium currency) - major gating mechanism.

INFRASTRUCTURE FABRIALS:

Heatrial System (Cost: 3 Gemhearts)
- Bonus: 1.5x total Provision cap multiplier (stackable)
- Formula: base_cap × (1.5 ^ quantity_owned)
- Impact: Enables exponential army growth with ownership

Synchronized Ledger (Cost: 3 Gemhearts)
- Bonus: 1.5x Market income (stackable)
- Formula: (400 × markets) × (1.5 ^ quantity_owned)
- Economic multiplier: Passive income acceleration

Gravity-Spanning Rig (Cost: 5 Gemhearts)
- Bonus: 2.0x Army speed/mission duration reduction (stackable)
- Formula: duration / (2.0 ^ quantity_owned)
- Impact: Missions complete in half time, enables faster rotations

ARENA FABRIALS (Combat-specific):

Regeneration Plate (Cost: 1 Gemheart)
- Bonus: +1 Champion HP healing (once per day, manual)
- Champion-only system

Thrill Amplifier (Cost: 2 Gemhearts)
- Bonus: Trade 1 HP for +3 Thrill in combat (once per day)
- Arena-only mechanic

Half-Shard (Cost: 4 Gemhearts)
- Bonus: Block one attack in combat (once per day)
- Defensive arena tool

Logic: Fabrials are long-term investments, require planning

8. PLAYER PROGRESSION SYSTEM
================================================================================

GATING PROGRESSION:
Start → Research Library Unlock → Fabrials Accessible → 
Territory Expansion → Equipment Unlock → End-game Scaling

PRESSURE POINTS (Where progression slows):
1. Early: Provision cap (requires Soulcast Bunkers)
2. Mid: Gemheart scarcity (limits fabrial access)
3. Late: Land expansion (conquest gets progressively harder)
4. End-game: Gemheart income (plateaus, farming intensifies)


================================================================================
9. TIME MECHANICS
================================================================================

DAY CYCLE:
- Dev mode: 10 seconds = 1 day
- Production: 1 hour = 1 day
- Predictable for testing, meaningful for players

MISSION DURATION CALCULATION:
base_duration × speed_multiplier × (1 / gravity_rig_multiplier) = actual_hours
Example: Scout (1 day) × 1.0 × (1 / 2.0 with rig) = 12 hours

Time gates progression, forces strategic deployment timing


================================================================================
10. BALANCE FRAMEWORK
================================================================================

CORE BALANCE DIMENSIONS:

Resource Scarcity: Spheres < Provisions < Gemhearts (least scarce to most)
Progression Time: Early access to abundance, mid-game rationing
Power Scaling: Linear (buildings) vs Exponential (fabrials)
Decision Points: Unit composition, building prioritization, mission selection

TENSION POINTS REQUIRING BALANCE:
- Too many spheres = game too easy, fabrials not valued
- Too few gemhearts = progression frustrating, whales only
- Unit costs vs provision cap = early game gate
- Mission rewarding vs duration = time investment ROI
- Casualty rates = high-risk strategy viability
